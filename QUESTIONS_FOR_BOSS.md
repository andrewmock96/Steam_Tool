# Questions Before Launch

## Branding
- What should the tool be called publicly? (Steam Research, Steam Market Intelligence, SteamLens, something else?)
- Do we have a logo or should we design one?
- Should it match PaperOS brand colors/style?
- Do we need a custom domain? (e.g. steamresearch.paperos.com)

## PaperOS Integration
- What URL should the CTA link to? (paperos.com, a custom landing page for game devs?)
- What CTA copy do you want? Draft: "When you're ready to protect that revenue with proper contracts and legal structure, PaperOS can help."
- Where should the CTA appear? (game detail panel, market overview, both?)
- Should we track CTA clicks for analytics?

## AI Assistant
- Do we have an Anthropic API key to use? (PaperOS may already have one)
- Any budget cap or rate limiting for API usage?

## Data & Legal
- Do we need a terms of service or disclaimer page?
- Are we comfortable with the current "Estimates based on Steam PC sales only" disclaimer?
- Should we add a privacy policy? (no user data collected currently, but the AI chat sends messages to Anthropic)

## Hosting & Launch
- Is Render free tier fine for launch, or should we upgrade? (free tier sleeps after 15 min inactivity)
- Who is the target audience for the initial launch? (indie dev communities, Reddit, Twitter?)
- Any other PaperOS team members who should review before launch?
